campus life

history of the school in 2003

now at 2003

The innings began with the inauguration of our school by Anil Kumble on June 8th 2003.

history of the school in 2004

now at 2004

Deccan International School was affiliated with the ‘Council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations’ (ICSE)

history of the school from 2006-2008

now from 2006-2008

Sports, cultural and literary events started to become a regular affair with students representing our school and winning prizes at major competitions. Music Enrichment classes were introduced for the first time.

history of the school in 2010

now at 2010

Deccan International School joined an elite group of schools when we were awarded ISA accreditation in November. From December onwards open schooling became a part of the Deccan International School through the NIOS Program

history of the school in 2011

now at 2011

Technology entered the classrooms in 2011 and e-learning brought changes in how teachers taught and students learnt with the introduction of Educomp Smartclass. This year also saw our school getting affiliated to the IGCSE, which now allows our students to opt for a Cambridge Certification.

history of the school in 2012

now at 2012

The swimming pool – a long cherished dream, became a reality with the inauguration of the Nettakallappa Aquatic Centre.

history of the school in 2013

now at 2013

After a decade, through an extensive re-branding exercise, Deccan International School has redefined its Vision and Mission, keeping in mind the best outcome for the child. This was unveiled to the parents during the Annual Days in November.

achievements

Inter School Dog and Bone contest

In my inter school dog and the bone contest, I won. I got a 1st place certificate. It was on sports day. It was a lot of fun. I felt very happy when I got the certificate. I was proud of myself.






Inter School Olympiad

In my inter school Olympiad, we had three Olympiads. We had Maths, Science and English. I got Silver in Science Olympiad. I was very happy. I did not think that I would get silver. I was very happy and proud of myself.






Inter School Fancy Dress Competition

In my inter school Fancy Dress Competition, I had dressed up as a cowboy. The fancy dress competition was a lot of fun! I did not get 1st, 2nd or 3rd prize but I got 4th prize. Even though I got 4th prize, I was still happy.
